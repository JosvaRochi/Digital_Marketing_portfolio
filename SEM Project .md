## Project Overview
This project focuses on creating and structuring a Google Ads (Search) campaign to drive relevant traffic for a travel company offering leisure and luxury tours in India.

## Objectives
- Drive targeted traffic through Google Search Ads  
- Increase visibility for travel-related keywords  
- Create a structured and scalable SEM campaign  

## Work Done
- Created Google Ads account in expert mode  
- Planned campaign structure with multiple ad groups  
- Keyword research and selection  
- Used different keyword match types (Broad, Phrase, Exact)  
- Created multiple text ads for each ad group  
- Defined campaign objectives and targeting  

## Tools Used
- Google Ads  
- Google Keyword Planner  

## Outcome
- Developed a well-structured Google Ads search campaign  
- Improved understanding of SEM campaign planning and execution  
- Created ads aligned with user intent and keywords
